About Halmgaard Wiki

Last updated: February 2026

Halmgaard Wiki is a fan-made, independently maintained wiki dedicated to documenting and organizing information about the Halmgaard universe. It was created to provide a centralized resource for fans, players, and newcomers alike.

Our Mission

Our goal is to provide accurate guides, detailed character and item information, and an easy-to-navigate reference for the community. We aim to keep all information up-to-date and reliable for both casual users and enthusiasts.

What We Offer

  • Comprehensive guides and tutorials
  • Detailed entries on characters, locations, and items
  • Tips, strategies, and community resources
