Event Information

Christmas Event Rewards

During the holiday event, Red Gifts can drop from defeating any mob in the game. Bring your Red Gifts to Santa in the main city to exchange them for exclusive rewards!

Santa Outfit
Trade Red Gifts for this festive look!
Santa Ring
Exclusive event accessory
Santa Backpack
Holiday-themed backpack
Candy Cane
Holiday-themed consumable
  • Candycane: Dropped by mobs during the event. This consumable gives your character a temporary buff!

Tip: Every mob in the world has a chance to drop Red Gifts and Candycanes during the event. Visit Santa in the main city to trade your Red Gifts for the Santa Outfit, Santa Ring, and Santa Backpack!

How to Participate

Look for the Santa NPC in the main town square during the holiday period. Complete their quests and defeat event monsters to collect Red Gifts. The more you collect, the more rewards you can claim!
